new Endboss()

Description:
  • Class representing the final boss enemy. Extends MoveableObject and manages multiple animation states, including walking, alert, attack, hurt, and death. The Endboss also maintains its own health (`energyEndBoss`) and contact status.

Endboss

new Endboss()

Description:
  • Creates a new Endboss instance. Loads all animation images, initializes speed and position, and starts the behaviour and animation loops. The boss does NOT use gravity here and stays on a fixed Y-level defined by Endboss#y. Jumps are animated by temporarily modifying his Y-position and then returning to baseY.

  • models/endboss.class.js, line 243
